🔴 Problem 1: Ads Spending Without Purchases

  • 15 ads spent 500+ EGP with 0 purchases
  • Total wasted spend: 16,736 EGP
  • They generate clicks but fail to convert

🔴 Problem 2: Low Hook Rate

  • Most ads have a hook rate below 20%
  • 80% of viewers swipe past in the first 3 seconds
  • Cause: opening doesn't address a pain point directly

🔴 Problem 3: Poor Video Completion

  • Completion rate is very low across most video ads
  • CTA is at the end — if nobody reaches it, no conversion
  • Fix: use 15-second format or place CTA in the middle

🔴 Problem 4: Creative Fatigue

  • Same people see the same ad 2+ times
  • Need fresh creatives every 2–3 weeks

🔴 Problem 5: No Retargeting Stack

  • 989 cart abandoners with no follow-up
  • 308 checkout abandoners — near-purchase intent lost
  • No dedicated retargeting campaigns visible

🔴 Problem 6: Single-Campaign Dependency

  • 81% of budget in one campaign
  • No diversification or risk management
  • Each variation needs its own adset to test properly

Section 8

✅ Recommended Solutions

✅ Fix 1: Immediately Pause Losing Ads

  • Pause any ad that spent 3× CPP with 0 purchases
  • Free up 16,736 EGP to reallocate
  • Redirect budget to proven winner creatives

✅ Fix 2: Scale Winner Creatives

  • "new mats – painting": +50% budget every 3–5 days
  • "New Sales Ad": build 3–5 new variations
  • "Flexible – Copy 3": test in Broad + LLK audiences

✅ Fix 3: Build Retargeting Stack

  • Adset 1: ATC last 7 days — 10% discount limited offer
  • Adset 2: Checkout last 3 days — urgency message
  • Adset 3: View Content 3 days — social proof creative

✅ Fix 4: Improve Hook Rate

  • First 3 seconds: direct question or problem — no intro
  • Make 3 versions of each creative with different hooks
  • Target: Hook Rate above 30% before continuing spend

✅ Fix 5: Diversify Campaign Structure

  • 70% Prospecting split across 2–3 campaigns
  • 20% Retargeting (ATC + Checkout + VC)
  • 10% Lookalike Audiences from Buyers

✅ Fix 6: Checkout Page Optimization

  • A/B test the checkout page layout
  • Add "Cash on Delivery" visibly above the buy button
  • Add trust badges: guarantee + reviews + free shipping
Section 9

💡 New Creative Ideas

Based on creative performance analysis, identified funnel drop-offs, and ad format patterns — 8 new creative concepts with their funnel impact:

🎥 Unboxing Surprise

15-sec short video — hands opening The Mat box, mat revealed. Hook: 'What's inside will transform your home'

🎯 Hook Rate 💡 Most ads have Hook < 15% — this fixes it
👩‍💼 UGC Real Room

Real customer films at home, places the mat, shares honest reaction. 30 seconds.

🎯 ATC → Purchase 💡 Authentic trust raises Checkout CVR
⚡ Before/After 6s

6 seconds: plain room → same room with the mat added. Reels format.

🎯 Scroll-Stop / Hook 💡 Fastest hook possible for cold audience
📊 Social Proof Counter

Static: '2000+ Egyptian families chose The Mat' + order collage.

🎯 Checkout → Purchase 💡 Breaks the 'unsure about quality' objection
🎯 Problem/Solution

Hook: 'Your rug collects dust and looks dirty?' → Solution: The Mat washable. 20 sec.

🎯 ATC → Checkout 💡 Addresses pain point directly
🏠 Room Tour Format

Quick tour across 3 rooms — each with a different mat + overlay price.

🎯 VC → ATC 💡 Showcases catalog, lifts ATC rate
💬 WhatsApp Review

Screenshot-style: real WhatsApp chat from a happy customer. Static.

🎯 Checkout → Purchase 💡 Social proof at near-zero production cost
🔄 Comparison Grid

Static: The Mat vs ordinary rug — 5 points (price / cleaning / looks / durability / delivery).

🎯 ATC → Checkout 💡 Justifies price and speeds up decision

📋 Production Priority

Priority 1: Before/After 6s + Unboxing (fastest to produce, highest hook potential)
Priority 2: UGC Real Room + WhatsApp Review (trust builders)
Priority 3: Room Tour + Comparison Grid (catalog + decision makers)

📐 Production Specs

Format: 9:16 (Reels / Stories primary)
Duration: 6–20 seconds (no longer)
Subtitles: mandatory (80% watch without sound)
CTA: at second 5–8, not the end

🎯 Scale Criteria

Hook Rate > 25%
P50 (midpoint view) > 40%
CPP in first 3 days < 1,200 EGP
ROAS in first week > 3x
